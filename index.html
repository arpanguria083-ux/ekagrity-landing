FAQ

Common questions

Does it work offline?

Yes. The timer, goals, and basic analytics all work without an internet connection. AI coaching and cloud sync require a connection.

What is BYOK?

Bring Your Own Key. Pro users can connect their own Google Gemini API key for unlimited AI usage with Gemini 2.5 Pro — their quota, their cost.

Is my data private?

Session data is synced to your account via Convex. Your Gemini API key (BYOK) is sent directly to Google and is never stored on our servers.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account settings at any time. You keep access until the end of your billing period, then revert to the Free plan.

Which browsers are supported?

Ekagrity is a Chrome extension and works on any Chromium-based browser: Chrome, Brave, Edge, Arc, and Opera.

What AI model powers the coach?

Free and Plus users get Gemini 2.0 Flash (fast and capable). Pro users get Gemini 2.5 Pro — Google's most powerful reasoning model.
